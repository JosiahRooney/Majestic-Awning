About Us

Welcome to our site! Doug Cromley, the owner of Majestic Awning & Canopy, began in the awning business in 1983. Family owned and operated, Majestic Awning & Canopy was established in 2000 and serves all of Sonoma County, as well as areas of Northern Marin and Southern Mendocino counties.

We take great pride in our craftsmanship, timeliness and unparalleled customer service. Doug cares about his customers and that is apparent from your first call to us for a free estimate (or maybe just some advice about your current shade, rain or sun protection need).

We look forward to hearing from you! Call us today!
